About Me

Hello! i’m Er. G. Shankar Sah.

Originally from Nepal, I am a computer engineer with a strong focus on cybersecurity, artificial intelligence/machine learning (AI/ML), and Python development. In addition to my cybersecurity work, I actively explore AI/ML technologies to create intelligent, adaptive security solutions. My combined skills in cybersecurity, Python programming, and AI/ML enable me to build forward-thinking systems that address the evolving landscape of digital threats.
